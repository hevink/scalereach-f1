## Competitor Deep Dive

### Opus Clip
- **Pros**: Strong AI clipping, good virality scoring, B-roll generator
- **Cons**: Expensive ($29/mo for 300 credits), limited storage (30 days), no split screen
- **Best for**: Podcasters who need auto-import from YouTube

### Klap
- **Pros**: AI dubbing in 29 languages, good clip generation
- **Cons**: Very expensive ($63/mo for 30 videos), video-based limits not minutes
- **Best for**: Multi-language content creators

### Vizard
- **Pros**: Good free tier (60 min), reasonable pricing
- **Cons**: Limited AI features, no split screen, basic backgrounds
- **Best for**: Budget-conscious beginners

### Submagic
- **Pros**: Good caption templates, trendy styles
- **Cons**: No AI clipping (captions only), 50MB file limit, short video max (30 min)
- **Best for**: Caption-focused editing only

### 2short.ai
- **Pros**: Cheapest option ($9.90/mo)
- **Cons**: Limited features, YouTube-focused only, basic editing
- **Best for**: YouTube Shorts creators on tight budget

### VEED.io
- **Pros**: Full video editor, many AI tools
- **Cons**: Not specialized for clipping, expensive for full features
- **Best for**: General video editing (not clipping-specific)

### Wisecut
- **Pros**: Good auto-silence removal, social hub included
- **Cons**: Expensive Pro tier ($75/mo), no AI clipping
- **Best for**: Podcast editing with auto-silence removal
